--- layout: content id: membership nav: true nav-order: 2 title: Become a Member intro: Membership with English Heritage offers you unlimited access to over 400 historical places with over 800 events for a whole year. bg-color: "#242518" features: - id: places title: Unlimited entry to over 400 historic places description: >

English Heritage cares for more than 400 historic places – from world-famous prehistoric sites to grand medieval castles, and from Roman forts on the edges of the empire to Cold War bunkers. As soon as you join, you’ll be able to interact with 5,000 years of England’s history, all year round.

- id: events title: Hundreds of historic events description: >

Evade the clash of swords, re-live battles and watch as soldiers fight head on in our dramatic re- enactments. Step back in time and discover life through the ages, from the sights and sounds of the medieval encampments to the discoveries made in Victorian Britain. Little ones can get hands-on with history with craft workshops and discovery trails for intrepid adventurers.

- id: kids title: Kids go free description: >

Step outside the classroom and see eyes light up as kids get hands-on with history. As part of your membership, you can take up to six children under 19 for free with every adult Member, as long as they’re in your family group. They’ll explore castle secrets, dress-up in authentic clothing and meet historical characters. There’s plenty to do, come rain or shine. With over 400 exciting places to visit across the country, you’ll never be short of great value days out with the whole family.

- id: magazine title: Members’ Magazine and handbook description: >

As a Member, you will receive the English Heritage handbook for free every year. Inside the handbook you’ll find all the inspiration and information you need to plan an entertaining day out, take a trip to a previously unseen site, discover recently opened rooms and exhibitions and gain a fresh insight into the past.

Delivered four times a year, the exclusive Members’ Magazine is packed with inspiration and ideas for making the most of your English Heritage membership.

- id: attractions title: Discounted attractions and exclusive offers description: >

Included with membership, you can enjoy free admission (half price for Members in their first year) to hundreds more historic places cared for by Cadw, Historic Scotland, OPW Heritage Ireland, Manx National Heritage and Heritage New Zealand.

Plus, Members receive discounted entry at our associated attractions, ranging from Museums and stunning Georgian Gothic architecture, to stately World Heritage Sites and inspiring Roman Palaces.
